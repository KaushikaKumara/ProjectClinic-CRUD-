If you are not an already registered user, click the "Not Registered User" button to register to the system by putting your details.

Once you filled your details correctly it will direct you to the login page again.

Then you have to fill up the name that you have given and your generated patient Id, when you were registering to the system.

If you have registered yourself already, you can click the "Registered User" button and fill up your name and the Id for the login process.

When you are logged in, the website will show you three main options such as Clinic, Pharmacy and Booked appointments.
